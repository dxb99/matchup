How the Matchup Generator Works

The DXB99 Matchup Generator creates balanced team matchups based on the players selected for that session.

First, the Match Maker selects their name and unchecks the players who are not present. When the Generate Matchup button is pressed, the generator displays all possible team combinations.

Each combination is evaluated based on the total skill of the players on each team. The difference between the two team totals is called the Skill Gap.

You can use the Skill Gap filters to narrow down the results. Lower gaps create more balanced matches, while higher gaps allow more variation in team strength.

Once a matchup is selected, it becomes the current matchup for everyone viewing the app and remains active for a limited time before expiring.

The generator also keeps a history of previous matchups and tracks how many times each team combination has been selected.
